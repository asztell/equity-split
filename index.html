Equity-Split solution accurately converts productivity and contribution into Equity Share ownership.

Teamwork, Clarity, Trust





Equity Share Metric

Equity Share algorithm is measuring ongoing work and translating it into individual share ownership. Member who works and contributes more will be rewarded more. Algorithm accounts three contribution categories and several variables.

Team building

Team can concentrate on building and developing business when equity share is fairly calculated. We have seen startups failing to focus on their day-to-day work due to inability to fairly and clearly articulate how to measure member's contribution. Our solution will allow your team to avoid such issues and establish teamwork from day one as everyone`s input will be transparent.

Productivity

How to measure every team member contribution and productivity? Is it initial idea or experience? Is it work done or hours spent? How to properly estimate it and reward all participants? Productivity is measured by capturing hours spent to the projects and value that each participant brings to the table.
